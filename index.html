About Me

My name is Wei Jiang. I was born on June 14 1998, in GuangZhou China but raised in Philadelphia
majority of my life. Throughout my life I had a good amount of ups and downs, despite my young age.
One of the downs I had in my life, that I currently and forever will regret was my poor decision
on not finishing up High School. I gave up during half way into the semester of my senior year,
when I should of persevere through it, but instead I fell into a slumb, where I gave myself endless
excuses and lazyness. Thankfully I was able to push myself out of that slumb a year later.



Now I am a Realtor at Cannan Realty and a Project Manager/Owner of a small start up construction business,
Jiang's Construction Services with my father with over 15 years of construction experience.
